What should I talk about?

Communicate, Coach, Care

Communicate

  • Communicate with your employee that you are concerned about their well being.
  • Make this a positive call. Stress that your number one concern is for their safety and you are glad that they are okay.
  • The goal of the conversation is not to find fault. It is to help drivers be as safe and productive as possible on the
    • road.

Coaching Topics / Talking Points

    Personal – Financial Issue

  • Talking to a driver about these sorts of issues requires a great deal of care and concern
  • Is there anything that has happened recently that is bothering you?
  • Has the company met your expectations for miles and trips in the past few weeks?
  • Are there any additional resources the company could provide to make you feel more secure with your work?
  • How are things at home? o Any major life changes recently? o Financial concerns?

    • Care

  • Close the call on a positive note. Let your driver know you are there to help them in the future.
  • Your number one concern is their safe arrival and personal safety.
  • Let your employee know if they ever feel they are not getting proper support from the company that they should contact you.
  • Follow-up with the driver in a week to see how they are progressing with any items discussed during the conversation.
